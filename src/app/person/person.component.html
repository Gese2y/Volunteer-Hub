Rights and Benefits of a Volunteer

Any person, who is willing to accept and translate it to deeds the fundamental principles of the society may become a volunteer of the society without discrimination related to race, color, nationality, sex, religion, political opinion or any other status. A volunteer may:

  • Be eligible for election in the governance of the society at all levels
  • Volunteering connects you to others.
  • Volunteering is good for your mind and body.
  • Volunteering can advance your career.
  • Volunteering brings fun and fulfillment to your life.
